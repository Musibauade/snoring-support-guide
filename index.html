The Solution

7 steps to silent sleep

Each step is backed by clinical research and can be started tonight — no equipment required for the first three.

01
🛌
Sleep on Your Side

Sleeping on your back lets gravity collapse your tongue into your airway. Side sleeping alone eliminates snoring in 70% of position-dependent snorers.

⏱ Results: 1–2 nights
02
🍷
Cut Alcohol 3+ Hours Before Bed

Alcohol relaxes throat muscles far beyond normal sleep relaxation. Even people who don't usually snore will snore after drinking. The fix? Stop drinking at least 3 hours before sleep.

⏱ Results: The very next morning
03
👅
Daily Throat Exercises

Just 10 minutes of targeted mouth and throat exercises daily strengthens the muscles that keep your airway open during sleep. Studies show a 36% reduction in snoring in 3 months.

⏱ Results: 2–4 weeks
04
👃
Clear Your Nasal Passages

Congested nasal passages force mouth breathing, which dramatically amplifies snoring. A simple saline rinse and bedroom humidifier can transform your sleep quality overnight.

⏱ Results: Same night
05
⚖️
Lose a Little Weight

Extra weight around the neck squeezes your airway shut during sleep. Losing even 5–10% of body weight can dramatically open your airway and reduce snoring frequency.

⏱ Results: 2–8 weeks
06
🌙
Build a Sleep Routine

Irregular sleep schedules cause chronic sleep debt, which deepens the sleep stages where snoring gets worst. A consistent bedtime trains your body to maintain better airway muscle tone.

⏱ Results: 5–7 days
07
🦷
Try Anti-Snoring Devices

For structural causes of snoring, a mandibular advancement device (MAD) gently moves your lower jaw forward during sleep — keeping your airway open all night. Works from the very first use.

⏱ Results: First night
